SALON
       CITRINE

- Jen Nickels, Stylist & Owner -

Salon Citrine is a single chair salon owned by longtime Lawrence stylist Jen Nickels offering cuts, color and facial waxing services to men and women.
Established in Lawrence, KS in 2017.
Jen is a friendly and energetic stylist who has been working behind the chair since 2007. Upon completion of cosmetology school, she embarked on a year long apprenticeship with intense Aveda and Vidal Sassoon training. Throughout the last decade, she has trained extensively through L'Oreal Professional, Morrocan Oil, and AlfaParf. Jen boasts extensive knowledge of color theory and color correction, and is highly educated and experienced in dry cutting and curly hair cutting. Relying on her own creativity, Jen strives to create a tailored look and experience to fit the personality of each of her guests. Jen believes in styling with an emphasis on client education, focusing on providing her customers with the products and styling knowledge necessary to recreate her styles. She takes great pride in her listening skills and customer service and goes out of her way to provide a relaxing atmosphere at Salon Citrine.
